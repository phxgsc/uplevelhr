Who We Are

Innovators, software engineers, data scientists, and data engineers, with experience at prominent Silicon Valley technology companies

We work with our customers to build data products, allowing businesses to take charge of their data and use it to gain a competitive advantage in the market.

Data Wrangling

We make your data usable. We are skilled in creating data lakes (both cloud and on-premise), automating the data acquisition process, data quality verification process, as well as data context enrichment and storage. We will work with you to make sure your domain knowledge is reflected in how we collect and organize data.

Data Analysis

Your data tells a story and interpreting this story correctly is a key to giving your business a competitive advantage. We have data scientists on staff who specialize in specific verticals to help you get most out of your data. We use the best tools available at our disposal, including supervised/unsupervised machine learning as well as deep learning.

Model Deployment

Analysis means nothing if you can't replicate it and reliably use it in production. We use best practices in continuous integration and continuous delivery to make sure your models continue to run and produce valid results long after our engagement is finished. We can do so for batch models, streaming models, and large-scale models that utilize cloud or on-premise distributed architectures.

Knowledge Transfer

Once everything is done, we will train your resident data scientists and software engineers in how to operate our data pipeline in production and what levers/parameters they can use to tune the models as your business cases evolve. We further have certified trainers on staff who can teach your staff the basics of data acquisition/processing, machine learning, model deployment, and building machine learning pipelines to help put our work into context.

What We Do

We have everything you need to start you on your data journey.

Our engagements generally fall within the following categories:

Data Science Strategy

We work with you to understand your business and your data and derive your sources of value. We then decompose our findings in terms of concrete project proposals, including potential schedules, resources, and technology choices, that can help you use data science and ML to create value for your business.

ML Data Pipelines

If you have existing analytics in place we work with your data and software engineers to build machine learning pipelines where we can automate training, scoring, and deployment, scale your models for large-scale analytics, and provide monitoring infrastructure to monitor for model effectiveness, efficiency, and concept drift.

Data Lake Creation

Best analytics are created from rich data, and rich data is data that has a lot of context. The best tool for creating such data sets are data lakes, where different data sets can live in once place and be re-processed multiple times as new insights are learned. We help you create such data lakes in the cloud (AWS, Azure, and Google) as well as on-prem (Hadoop-based technologies).

Data Project Management

Data science projects are hard to implement on time and on budget because (a) they often feel like a science experiments that never end and (b) they are often performed by academics without much production experience. We provide project managers with a track record of success on various high-profile data projects who can get the best out of your team and deliver on time and on budget.

Model UI/UX Design

Often a key aspect to interpreting a model is being able to interactively visualize its output. We work with several UI designers and engineers with experience specifically in creating data-driven visualizations to help make your models more interpretable and actionable. We can also use 3rd party visualization frameworks and libraries where appropriate for your use cases.

Model API Design

We help you create model interfaces to make them discoverable and usable by the rest of your organization. We typically design for the cloud and leverage the latest advancements in microservice and container technologies to scale out your models based on your use case. In case you want to monetize your data, we are skilled in cloud consumption models and can design your models and your APIs to conform with the latest best practices.
